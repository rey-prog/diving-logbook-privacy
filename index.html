Privacy Policy - Commercial Divers Logbook

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Commercial Divers Logbook ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Our App collects and stores the following types of information:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Profile Data: Full name, date of birth, address, contact information
  • Professional Information: Qualifications, certifications, professional details
  • Medical Information: Medical certificates, fitness certifications, medical notes, blood group, allergies, and other health-related information

2.2 Dive Log Data

  • Dive Records: Dive dates, locations, depths, times, equipment used
  • Project Information: Contractor details, vessel information, project descriptions
  • Technical Data: Decompression schedules, breathing mixtures, safety procedures

2.3 Media Content

  • Images: Certificate photos, qualification images, profile pictures
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings used for AI-assisted dive log entry

2.4 Usage Data

  • App Analytics: Feature usage, crash reports, performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide diving logbook features, store your records, and generate reports
  • AI Features: To process voice recordings and images for automated dive log entry
  • Data Management: To organize, search, and export your diving records
  • Statistics: To generate diving statistics and performance analytics
  • App Improvement: To improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • All personal and professional information
  • Medical certificates and qualifications
  • Complete dive log history
  • Photos and voice recordings

4.2 Security Measures

  • Data encryption for sensitive information
  • Secure local storage mechanisms
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers without explicit consent

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Voice recordings may be processed by AI services for transcription (data is not stored by these services)
  • App Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics may be shared with analytics providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Modification: Edit or update your information as needed
  • Deletion: Delete specific records or all data from the app
  • Export: Generate and share PDF reports of your data
  • Backup: Create backups of your data for personal use

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services or as required by law. You can delete your data at any time through the App's settings or by uninstalling the application.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

As your data is stored locally on your device, international data transfers are minimal. Any data processing for AI features is handled securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store Requirements
  • Apple App Store Guidelines
